Installing the skin:

1. 	Extract the skin to this folder :
	D:\Steam\skins (May differ on your PC)

2. 	Restart steam

3. 	Right click on the steam icon (taskbar), and select settings

4. 	Click on Interface

5. 	Select "SimpleSync" from the dropdown menu

6. 	Restart steam
